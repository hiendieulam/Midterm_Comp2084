BVLGARI. TRIBUTE TO FEMININITY MAGNIFICENT ROMAN JEWELS

7 September 2018 – 13 January 2019

For the first time in Russia, the Moscow Kremlin Museums will hold a retrospective exhibition, presenting pieces of high jewellery art by world-known Italian Maison BVLGARI, which history dates back more than a hundred and thirty years. Over four hundred unique pieces of high-end jewellery from the Bulgari Heritage Collection, as well as pieces from the private collections from around the globe, will be on display at two exhibition halls of the Kremlin museums — the Exhibition Hall of the Assumption Belfry and the Exhibition Hall of the Patriarch’s Palace.

BVLGARI. Tribute to Femininity. Magnificent Roman Jewels The Bulgari Heritage Collection and the Moscow Kremlin Museums present an exhibition catalogue "BVLGARI. Tribute to Femininity. Magnificent Roman Jewels" introducing pieces of high jewellery art by world-known Italian Maison BVLGARI, which history dates more than a hundred and thirty years back. The catalogue is on sale in our souvenir shops in the Alexander Garden, in the Armoury Chamber and in the shop situated opposite the Patriarch's Palace. Price - 4000 RUB.

Lucia Boscaini, Brand and Heritage Curator

“Preparing the retrospective, we considered Bvlgary in a special perspective, i.e. how women acquired more freedom, including freedom in jewellery. We aimed to trace jewellery becoming more flexible as it was following the development of Bvlgari jewellery art. We traced the fashion evolution of the second half of the 20th century and the way jewellery was blending with it.
The idea was to show either celebrated women or ordinary people—that’s why the exhibition banner represents just a model in the historical shooting, not a movie star. Everyone can see herself in this woman. Not only celebrities but any women wearing Bvlgari felt at ease as they perceived that jewellery as an honour to themselves. That is the key point of the show at the Kremlin, not just demonstrating beautiful stones combined in jewellery pieces.
